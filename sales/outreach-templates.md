## Pro Tips

1. **Personalize the first line.** Find something specific about them (recent post, company, achievement).

2. **Always include the demo link.** Visual proof  > words.

3. **Ask for 15 minutes, not an hour.** Low commitment = higher yes rate.

4. **Follow up 2-3 times.** Most people are just busy, not uninterested.

5. **Track everything.** Which template converts best? Double down on that.
