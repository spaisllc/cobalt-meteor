Frequently Asked Questions

How long does implementation take?

Most AI agents are live within 2-4 weeks. Complex custom solutions may take longer depending on integrations and training requirements.

What's the minimum commitment?

We offer month-to-month contracts after an initial 3-month period. This gives us time to optimize your agent for best results.

Do you offer a trial?

We offer a pilot program where we build a limited version of your agent so you can test performance before committing to a full deployment.

What AI models do you use?

We use the latest models from OpenAI (GPT-4) and Anthropic (Claude) depending on your use case. We can also work with other providers if required.

How do you handle data privacy?

Data privacy is paramount. We're SOC 2 compliant and can sign BAAs. Your customer data is never used to train models without explicit consent.

Can the AI integrate with my CRM?

Yes! We integrate with Salesforce, HubSpot, Pipedrive, and most major CRMs. We can also build custom integrations via API or Zapier.
