Common Questions

FAQ

What exactly is a "Talking Website"?

It's a website that speaks back. Instead of forcing visitors to read endless text, we embed a smart AI agent that can have a real, two-way voice conversation with them. It answers questions, explains your work, or captures leads‚Äîjust like a human would.

Is this just a chatbot?

No. Chatbots are text-only and usually frustrating. Our agents use advanced Voice AI to speak with human-like intonation, emotion, and intelligence. It feels like talking to a real person.

Do I need to know how to code?

Not at all. We handle the entire setup. We build the "Brain," train the voice, and give you a simple code snippet to drop onto your site (or we host the page for you).

What is a "Talking Resume"?

It's an interactive version of your CV. Instead of a recruiter skimming a PDF, they can talk to your AI agent. It can answer questions like "Tell me about a time you showed leadership" using context we program into it.

Can it sound like me? (Voice Cloning)

Yes! For our Personal Brand and Enterprise plans, we offer a "Digital Twin" add-on ($149). You record a few minutes of audio, and we clone your voice so your agent sounds exactly like you.

What if I get a job? Can I cancel?

Of course. The Talking Resume plan ($19/mo) is month-to-month. Use it to land the gig, then cancel. Or, upgrade to a Personal Brand site to keep networking while you work.

How does the "Business Greeter" capture leads?

When a customer asks about a service, the AI can say: "I can definitely help with that. What's the best phone number for us to reach you at?" It then emails that lead to you instantly.

Can it handle my scheduling?

Yes. We can instruct the agent to direct qualified leads to your Calendly or booking link.

Is 200 minutes enough for my business?

For most local businesses, yes. That's over 3 hours of pure conversation time per month. Since most customer questions are answered in 1-2 minutes, that covers about 100-150 customers.

What happens if I run out of minutes?

No stress! Your agent keeps talking. You simply pay a flat rate of $0.20/minute for the extra time. If you go viral, your agent scales with you. We call it "Viral Insurance."

Why do you charge for minutes?

Real-time Voice AI is computationally expensive. We pay for the transcription, the intelligence (GPT-4), and the voice synthesis for every second of conversation. Our plans cover these costs so you don't have to worry about the tech.

Is there a contract?

Only if you want a discount! Our monthly plans are pay-as-you-go with no commitment. If you choose an Annual Plan, you save ~50% but commit to 12 months.

How long does setup take?

Talking Resume: 24-48 hours. Business Greeter: 3-5 days. Custom Enterprise: 2+ weeks depending on complexity.

Does it work on mobile?

Yes. The voice widget works natively on iOS and Android browsers. No app download required.

Can I upgrade my plan later?

Absolutely. Start with a Talking Resume, grow into a Personal Brand Agent. Your AI learns and grows with you ‚Äî we just unlock more capacity.
